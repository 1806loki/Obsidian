The sky brightened each day, thinking of the sun as its forever companion.

Even after the sun left the sky alone every night, the sky couldn’t stop thinking of the sun.

Morning came again, and so did the sun.

But some days, the sun stayed hidden, leaving the sky cloudy and gray, even during the day.

Still, the sky waited.

And when the sun returned, the sky cleared and shone bright once more.

Until one final day, the sun left for good.

The sky was left alone, in the cold, dark night—forever
