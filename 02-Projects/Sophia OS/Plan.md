#project/Sophia
**Objective:**  
Create a system that helps users learn and achieve their goals by generating personalized learning plans, recommending resources, and providing ongoing feedback based on user input and progress.
App is a **"The Personal Exploration Hub"**, a place where users:
- Discover new topics
- Dive into deep and thought-provoking content
- Expand their worldview by learning things outside of traditional job skills
- Connect with others who share similar interests
- Cultivate a lifelong love for learning, exploration, and self-discovery.

---

### **Key Features:**

1. **Data Integration**  
    The system will integrate and store data from:
    
    - **Links** (websites, articles, videos)
        
    - **Documents** (Google Docs, Sheets, Notion)
        
    - **Chats** (conversations, feedback)
        
    - **Books & Notes** (reading lists, user notes)
        
2. **Personalized Learning Paths**  
    Users can set goals (e.g., “Improve communication skills” or “Learn about criminal psychology”) and the system will:
    
    - Suggest **resources** (books, videos, articles)
        
    - Create a **custom syllabus** for daily or weekly learning
        
    - Track **progress** and suggest **tasks** for improvement
        
3. **Feedback & Assessment**  
    The system will:
    
    - Grade progress and provide **weekly feedback**.
        
    - Suggest ways to improve, based on user performance and self-assessment.
        
4. **Resource Recommendations**  
    Based on your learning goals, the system will:
    
    - Recommend **books, videos, and websites** tailored to your interests.
        
    - Offer **interactive exercises** to reinforce key concepts.
        
5. **User Customization**  
    Users can:
    
    - Adjust the **duration** of learning periods (e.g., daily, weekly).
        
    - Choose the **depth** of feedback and recommendations (detailed or minimal).
        
6. **Simple UI**  
    The system will have a clean, easy-to-navigate interface, showing:
    
    - **Progress dashboard** with tasks, feedback, and suggestions.
        
    - **Reminders** for tasks and upcoming activities.
        
7. **AI Integration**  
    Using **AI (Large Language Models)**, the system will:
    
    - Automatically suggest resources based on user preferences.
        
    - Create dynamic **learning plans** and provide personalized feedback.
        

---

### **Privacy & Security:**

- Data will be **securely stored** and **encrypted**.
    
- Users will have control over what data is shared and stored.
    

---

### **Additional Considerations:**

- **Offline Access** to learning plans and resources.
    
- **Performance**: The system should handle large amounts of data efficiently.
    
- **Integrations** with other tools (e.g., calendar, task manager) for better planning.
